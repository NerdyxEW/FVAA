1. how might you combine predictions from multiple knn models?
- majority vote across models with different k values, distance metrics, or feature subsets
- bagging: train each model on a different bootstrap sample of the data

2. what benefits could this provide over a single knn?
- reduces sensitivity to choice of k and noisy neighbours
- more stable, less variance in predictions overall

3. what challenges or trade-offs might arise?
- knn is already slow; multiple models multiplies the cost significantly
- harder to interpret than a single knn
- diversity is harder to achieve with knn than tree-based models
